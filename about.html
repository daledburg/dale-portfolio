About Me

Why Change Now?

My name is Dale Dahlenburg and I'm determined to be a full stack web developer of the future. I am currently 27 years old and the idea of a career in the tech industry was never something I thought could be a possibility. After 10 years out of school pursuing various studies and careers, I finally stumbled upon the possibility of a career that truly interested me and I could see myself thriving in. After discovering the concept of bootcamps I researched many different providers and came to the amazing decision of enrolling with Coder Academy. The rest will be history.
